Information

What is the MSA?

The MSA stands for Muslim Student Association. It is a club that is held every Thursdays after school 3-5 PM.

Our goal

Our goal is to spread the kind message of Islam and offer a safe space for ALL backgrounds.

What we do

Our Club leaders work closely with our Club Advisor to make every club meeting a FUN and informational meeting.

Meetings

The MSA meets every Thursday 3PM-5PM. Feel free to stop by ANYTIME and you can leave ANYTIME you want. Everyone is welcome no matter their faith, race, and gender.
